Project Idea

Overview: For the project idea that we will be doing in a group I was thinking to make a game like dota but with cross-platform play. People can play it from any device even from their desktop PC's to tv with apple tv. They can even play it on their iPhones and Android devices even while they're not at home. It should be a strategy based game aimed at older people who don't play video games. that's why we will make it easy to play and aimed specifically for the elders but it can be played for all ages. It should be addictive, fun and good at making the player think strategically.

Motivation: The Motivation here is to start getting older people to start playing games from anywhere. This is to get them to think, strategize and have fun. Also, there have been some studies where elderly people who play video games even just occasionally are more social and less likely to develop depression than those who don't play. Some studies were linked to their happiness and Energy for those who did play. We want them to be more alert and also getting use to learning new technologies as we update the game to improve the experience.

Destription: It's a cross-platform game so it can be played from any hardware the player wishes. When they first download it to where ever they want they will be introduced to the rules of the game that are laid out very simply for them. We might even tell them its a game aimed for elderly people but remember anyone can play it. after they have read the rules written simply which shouldn't take long because they probably don't want it to be hard since they don't play any video games they will begin to practice the game. Each step they take in the game should be very easy at first then gradually start to get harder with each step. The game itelf is a 7 vs 7 online or offline strategy game where the goal is to level up your character and destroy the enemy teams base to win. the controls of the game are very simple but an advanced player who played similar games like this can change the controls and customize them to his or her preferred way of playing. they will have to enter their age and name when they first begin the tutorial and practice lesson. They will be asked some questions about how much video games they play and their language and most importantly their age. if they say that they have never played video games before and there over 23 the game will automatically give them the easiest control layout to play with. If they say they're over 30 and never played video games in their life we will show them a message then a video about why it's actually good to play video games. the video and messages will tell them the benefits that video games are having in adults and elderly people. But what about those who were younger and wanted to see the video about why games are beneficial for you? well, they don't see it if they want to see it it's in the main menu where they can watch it if they wish. they'll be introduced to an easy to use but an effective character that will help them make get used to the mechanics of the game. They will be taught how to purchase new items new gear for their character to make them stronger and why this is needed to win or increase the chances of winning. Also, they need to be able to work and communicate with other teammates that can be either set to an offline mode or an online mode if they want to do that.they can also choose how many players they want to vs and be teamed up with for example 2 v 2 or 4 v 4. An advanced player can even choose 1 v 5 or a 2 v 7 if he or she wants that challenge. Services with this product with cross-platform and frequent updates to add new characters and bug fixes to squish any bugs that are interfering with the game.

Tools and Technologies: The tools needed are software video game developers that will use suggest the preferred games software like unity or construct2 They need to be creative and come up with new ideas never seen before and different from dota or league of legends. The in-game environment should be 3d while using unity or another game development engine the developers will choose. C# should also be known to the game developers since it is the primary language used by the game engine. We also need to pay for the hardware like the servers and make sure they transmit enough data so there will be no delays from the player's inputs to the server.

Skills Required: The skills required and people needed for this project is especially video game developers. These developers must be experienced and have worked on a similar project like this before. we need people to design a beautiful but simple user interface that can be navigated around with no bugs occurring. this is when we need UI and UX designers that are also familiar and experienced with eliminating any bugs that happen. The characters will be designed in there own unique way by these designers working with the game developers very closely.

The Outcome: The Outcome we want is to see elderly people feel more alert, awake and happy and more importantly have fun. if the game is successful we will continue to support it with updates and releasing new characters with different skills to learn and play around with. The goal was to make the elderly to play from anywhere and with whatever hardware and technology they want to play it on. we will show advertisements and show who its aimed for and why.
